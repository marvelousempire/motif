# Motif Glossary

## Motif

A semantic lens placed over WooCommerce.

## Engine

The underlying WooCommerce and WordPress functionality that stores and organizes records.

## Lens

The visible interpretation of the engine.

## Semantic Mapping

The process of connecting an existing WooCommerce label to a new Motif label.

## Idea

The first Motif profile. An Idea is powered by a WooCommerce product underneath but presented with idea-centered language.

## Idea Trait

A remapped WooCommerce attribute.

## Idea Theme

A remapped WooCommerce tag.

## Idea Category

A remapped WooCommerce product category.

## Idea Version

A remapped WooCommerce variation.

## Feedback

A remapped WooCommerce review.

## Collection

A remapped cart-style experience.
