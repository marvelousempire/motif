# Motif

Motif means changing the visible lens while keeping the underlying engine.

Core phrase:

**Change the lens. Keep the engine.**

Motif is connected to [[WooCommerce Engine]], [[Semantic Mapping]], [[Idea Profile]], and [[RAG Strategy]].

## Meaning

Motif turns a WooCommerce product system into a flexible concept system.

The first concept system is Ideas.
