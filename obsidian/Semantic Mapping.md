# Semantic Mapping

Semantic Mapping is the process of translating one label into another meaning without changing the underlying function.

Example:

- Product becomes Idea
- SKU becomes Idea Reference ID
- Category becomes Idea Category
- Review becomes Feedback

This is the core method used by [[Motif]].

## Why It Matters

Semantic Mapping lets WooCommerce keep working normally while users experience a different system.
